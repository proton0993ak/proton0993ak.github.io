NOBILIS

At Nobilis Global, we are particularly proud of our global, integrated, transparent approach. Operating as one company, Nobilis leverages all business and partnering across the firms interests and on behalf of all fund investors and the companies who complete the investment projects. Nobilis’s Private Equity platform invests in and partners with industry-leading franchises and companies poised for significant improvement or growth that attract high-quality management teams and branded companies. Moreover, Nobilis attracts disciplined investors, focusing on long-term business fundamentals.

Nobilis Global looks to partner closely and cooperatively with management teams and entrepreneurs that have track records of success. The belief is the combination of our industry knowledge, investment experience, and operational expertise which provides Nobilis with an edge in identifying and creating value in investment opportunities. The diverse strategy is not only to work as partners with the management of portfolio companies, but also to assist in the operations of the businesses represented and leverage all of the resources of the global platform available to the company.

In addition to traditional management buyouts and build-ups, the company seeks to find opportunities to provide growth capital, as well as minority investments, and public toe hold investments where Nobilis can partner with public companies and leverage industry expertise and operational capabilities.


What makes NOBILIS “noble”?


NOBILIS delivers considerable return on investment within each area of our portfolio, with a constant focus on where its charitable donations from profits can have the most positive impact on the world. NOBILIS contributions to charity are carefully analyzed both before and after donation in an effort to ensure funds are used effectively and efficiently as intended. Our concern is sustainability for the cause, and NOBILIS is not about money given, it also serves to organize community action for a more productive society without leaving a footprint on their culture. NOBILIS serves the people, because NOBILIS is of the people.

Nobilis Social Responsibility

“To be noble” would have no meaning if Nobilis would not care about people, the cultures around the world, the environment and all socially conscious endeavors.

To the team Nobilis, all of these things matter. We feel it is our duty to “be” the world we want to see. Money, time and energy are all components of a good social responsibility corporate plan. Within this framework, Nobilis plans to launch a cohesive platform which enables and empowers others to feed themselves, empowers them to build
cottage industries, inhibit disease and utilize preventive health measures, while keeping the environment around them clean. This is because they have been educated in how just small things change not only their lives, but all who are around them.

Children are our future future, and Nobilis looks to a bright future for our children by empowering them to see a better world when they create the world peace for which we all yearn, along with the encouragement and guidance that a steady hand can provide. Nobilis yearns to answer to a world who needs those who care, those who see, and then those who respond to that call.

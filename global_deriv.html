NOBILIS GLOBAL DERIVATIVES

Nobilis Global is involved in core business areas including gold mining, diamond acquisition and distribution, corporate funding and real estate finance and development. It invests and funds in novel health products, noninvasive medical and alternative energy technology as well as funding health beauty and anti-aging companies, as well as in the development of high-end luxury earth friendly consumer products.

Founded this year to create 506B Reg D filing and SPV in the UK for its worldwide interests, Nobilis Global has offices and professional services in Nashville, Tennessee, New York, Switzerland, UK, Dubai and Hong Kong. The partners have combined business development and acquisition experience of 65 plus years focusing in the trading, marketing, branding, manufacturing and distribution industries.
